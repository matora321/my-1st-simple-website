Karachi Biryani

    Ingredients:-

  • Chicken
  • Rice , Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Green chili paste
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Oil

    Instruction:-
  • Marinate chicken
  • Fry onions and add ginger garlic paste and green chili paste
  • Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  • Add tomatoes, potatoes, rice, and water.
  • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cover.
  • Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until rice is cooked through.
  • Garnish and serve hot.

Enjoy!
