Burger recipe


    Ingredients:-

  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Buns
  • Toppings

    Instruction:-

  • Preheat grill or griddle.
  • Mix beef, salt, and pepper.
  • Form into patties and grill 4-5 minutes per side.
  • Toast buns.
  • Assemble burgers and serve.

Enjoy!
